Quality Education for the City's Children of Color

ongoing

The Black Institute is part of a campaign that advocates for initiatives with a proven track record of success for students from birth to college by pushing for the appropriate taxing the wealthiest 1%, thus creating $4 billion of funding for all students. The Black Institute has also collaborated to create surveys and collect data with a coalition concentrating on efforts to sustain the community school and renewal school initiatives in the city.
